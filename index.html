1. Overview

القراءات العشر (Ten Qiraah) is a Quran recitation app developed by Suleiman Darwish. This policy explains what data the app accesses, why, and how it is used. We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

This app does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or any account information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Audio recordings or voice data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to third parties
  • Any data transmitted to our servers

3. Permissions Used and Why

Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO)

Used exclusively for the voice recognition / recitation matching feature, which allows users to recite Quran and have the app highlight the matching ayah. Audio is processed entirely on-device using Android's built-in speech recognition. No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server. This permission is only active while the user explicitly starts voice recognition.

Internet (INTERNET)

Used to stream Quran audio files from trusted third-party recitation servers (e.g. mp3quran.net, everyayah.com). All connections use HTTPS (encrypted). No personal data is included in these requests — only the audio file URL is fetched.

Storage / Files

Used to save downloaded Quran audio files to your device so you can listen offline. Files are stored in the app's private storage folder and are not accessible to other apps.

Wake Lock

Used to keep the screen or processor active while audio is playing, so playback is not interrupted when the screen turns off.

4. Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • App settings and preferences (font size, theme, selected reciter)
  • Listening progress and bookmarks
  • Downloaded audio file references

This data never leaves your device. It is deleted when you uninstall the app.

5. Third-Party Services

The app streams audio from the following public Quran audio servers. These servers may log standard HTTP access logs (IP address, timestamp, file requested) as part of normal server operation. We have no control over these logs.

  • mp3quran.net
  • everyayah.com
  • server.mp3quran.net

No other third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks are used.

6. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
